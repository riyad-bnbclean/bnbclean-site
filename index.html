Install

Install it like a real app

Add BNB Clean to your home screen in seconds — no app store needed.

On iPhone

  1. Open the app in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button
  3. Choose “Add to Home Screen”
  4. Tap Add — done!
▲

On Android

  1. Open the app in Chrome
  2. Tap the menu (⋮ top right)
  3. Choose “Install app”
  4. Confirm — done!
ⓘ

Some Android phones (like Samsung) may show a notice that the app was “built for an older version of Android.” This is normal for web apps — just tap “Install anyway.” BNB Clean is completely safe.

About us

An app for the people

BNB Clean was built to be useful and fair to everyone — hosts who need a spotless place, and cleaners who deserve honest, on-time pay. We started small, in our own community, with a simple belief: a good clean and an honest wage should be easy to arrange. This is just the beginning.
